About Kanha fun city

Kanha Fun City is an amusement and water park located in the city of Bhopal, which was inaugurated in 2000. The park has a range of thrilling water rides, tube slides, roller coasters, rain dances, aqua disco arrangements etc. Kanha Fun City also has five venues that can host events such as wedding receptions, theme parties, corporate parties, school functions, picnics and product launches.
